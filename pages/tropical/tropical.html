Tropical Smoothie

Description

Smoothies are the language of summer! Seriously, who doesn't equate fruity drinks with the
warmest, sunniest, and fun-filled time of the year?

This tropical smoothie will send you on a one-way trip to Hawaii and have you saying Aloha!
The pineapple is what really sends it. Try the original recipe and then play around with the
proportions to suit your pallate.
