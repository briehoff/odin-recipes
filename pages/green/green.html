Green Smoothie

Description

Green smoothies are irreplacable in my opinion. They're great for getting a healthy serving or two
first thing in the morning. Gone are the days of endless salads to fill your daily quota of greens. A tasty
green smoothie makes it so much easier and enjoyable to get that healthy dose of vitamins and minerals.

This recipe is so easy and only a handful of ingredients making this the perfect morning smoothie
to start the day!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup almond milk or soy milk
  • 1/2 cups fresh spinach
  • 1 banana
  • 1 tablspoon peanut butter
  • 1 teaspoon honey (more or less to taste)
  • 5-6 ice cubes

Steps

  1. Add the almond milk, spinach, banana, peanut butter, and honey to a blender. Blend until smooth.
  2. Add the ice cubes and blend until smooth.
