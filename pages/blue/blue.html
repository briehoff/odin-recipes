Blue Smoothie Bowl

Description

This blue smoothie bowl hits the right spot! And look at how beautiful it looks! Seriously, this one is sure to be a hit.
It's refreshing, tasty, and healthy. This just gives off cotton candy vibes without all that sugar.

Make this to impress guests or as a personal guilt-free treat. I bet you'll never be the same.

Ingredients

  • 2 teaspoons spirulina (blue)
  • 2 bananas
  • 1 cup peaches
  • 1/4 cup pineapple (frozen)
  • 1 cup almond milk (or according to desired thickness)

Toppings:

  • 3 Blueberries (frozen)
  • 3 Blackberries
  • 1 tablspoon cocoa nibs
  • 1/4 banana (sliced)

Steps

  1. In a blender, mix frozen bananas and almond milk.
  2. Add the pineapple and peaches. Mix on low until blended.
  3. Add blue spirulina.
  4. Place in a bowl and add your favorite toppings.
